Location

Madurai is located at 9.93°N 78.12°E with an average elevation of 134 metres.

Vaigai River

River Vaigai runs in NorthWest - SouthEast direction dividing the city into 2 equal halves.

Neighbours

The city is surrounded by Dindigul, Trichy, Sivagangai, Theni and Virudhunagar.

Also called

The city Madurai also called as "Thoonga nagaram" (Sleepless city)

The city covers totally 3710 km2 and lies in SouthEast of Western Ghats.
